Elite Guide to XPForge

Welcome to XPForge, the accountability system where your real-world productivity fuels your digital ranking. This isn't just a list app—it's a battlefield. To dominate the leaderboards, you need strict discipline and strategic planning.

1. The XP Economy

Your primary objective is to gain XP (Experience Points). XP is the singular currency used to determine your rank, your power in Clan Battles, and your eligibility for Badges.

  • Planned Tasks
    Highest value targets. Log these before locking your plan.
    +7 XP
  • Failed Planned Tasks
    Broken promises penalize you permanently.
    −4 XP
  • Unplanned Tasks
    Unexpected crisis handling or minor bonus goals.
    +3 XP
Provisional vs Finalized XP

As you check off tasks, that XP is designated as "provisional" and added to your balance locally so you can see your active impact. However, the server runs a strict audit at exactly Midnight (UTC). Any planned tasks left unchecked at 11:59PM are immediately marked "Failed", subtracting −4 XP from your account before finalizing your score for the next day.

2. Daily Operations: The "Lock"

Every day requires a rigid commitment. Consistency is the only metric that matters.

  1. Drafting: Start your day by dumping your critical targets into the Planned Tasks list.
  2. Locking: To finalize your intent, press "Lock My Plan".
  3. No Turning Back: Once a plan is locked, you can no longer add new Planned tasks or delete existing ones. You must see them through to completion or accept the failure penalty.

Note: If unexpected work arises after locking your plan, drop it into the Unplanned Tasks input.

3. The Leaderboard Ecosystem

The objective of XP is dominance across 5 unique timelines. Your score automatically feeds into each one simultaneously:

Daily

Resets every night. High reflex speed required.

Weekly

Tracks the Mon-Sun ISO calendar.

Monthly / Yearly

Long-term grind boards tracking ultimate endurance.

Global

The all-time hall of fame.

4. Clans & Warfare

Being a lone wolf restricts your growth. In XPForge, forming a Clan unlocks the ability to wage war and claim significant bragging rights.

Starting or Joining a Clan
  • Creating: You can form a clan of up to 10 members.
  • Inviting: Copy the User ID at the bottom of a recruit's Profile page, and paste it into your Clan dashboard!
  • Joining: The recruit will receive an inline action button in their Notification Bell to Accept or Decline the request.
Clan Battles

Clans can challenge each other to fierce, time-limited skirmishes.

  1. Challenging: The Clan Leader requires the Clan ID of a rival clan.
  2. Duration: Choose to fight for 1-Day or 7-Days.
  3. The Goal: Both clans simply complete tasks. The total XP earned by all clan members combined during the battle window dictates the winner via a live Tug-of-War progress bar.
Beware the weak link!

If a member in your clan fails their planned tasks, their −4 XP penalty hurts the combined Clan score during an active battle! Choose your roster wisely and do not hesitate to kick inactive players.

5. Account Maintenance

  • Darkness or Light: Customize your aesthetic intensity. Tap the Sun/Moon icon in your top navigation bar to globally flip your terminal into Light Mode or Dark Mode.
  • Timezones: XPForge calculates the Midnight reset strictly based on Universal Time (UTC). However, inside your Profile panel, you can adjust your own timezone marker so external friends know where you belong.
